🧭 Mystic Trials ▼

PvE simulator where you fight fixed defender formations set by the trial stage. Import your "Battle Details" bonus screen from each trial report, pick the trial name (Crystal Cave, Molten Fort, etc.), and hit Run.

The engine scans 220+ attacker formations and recommends the one that deals maximum damage to the fixed defender. Each trial has different stat bonuses baked in — the optimal formation changes every time. The result card and scoreboard show exactly which formation to use before you actually march.

⚔ PvP Simulator ▼

Helps you plan the best troop formation to attack a specific real player's garrison. Two import paths:

  • Already fought the defender? One battle report screenshot fills everything automatically.
  • Scouting first? Open the scout report → tap the ! to see Troop Type Ratio → screenshot for defender troops. Scroll to bottom for Defender Stats screenshot. For your own stats: attack a Beast (solo march) or call a Rally on a Terror (rally march) and screenshot those Stat Bonuses.

The engine tests 220+ formations against the defender's exact army and ranks them — winners first, fewest own losses. Turn guesswork into a calculated strike.

🪖 Troops & Stats ▼

Your personal stats sheet — input your troop counts and stat bonuses across Infantry, Cavalry, and Archers. The data you enter here feeds directly into the Bear Magic and Bear Option-A calculators.

Acts as a single source of truth so you don't re-enter the same numbers on every tool. Keep it updated after each power-up or research unlock for accurate results. It's a quick form, not a simulator — enter once, benefit everywhere.

🧙 Heroes ▼

Catalogs every hero's battle skills and their effects on troop types during combat. Browse hero abilities, understand which buffs apply to Infantry, Cavalry, or Archers, and see how skills interact during battles.

It's a reference tool — no simulation here, just clean data about what each hero does. Use it when building a hero lineup to complement your preferred troop formation. Knowing your hero bonuses before running PvP simulations makes the results far more accurate.

🐻 Bear Magic ▼

Damage calculator specific to the Bear troop type used in special game modes. Input your bear's stats and available troops, and it scores different formation mixes to find maximum bear damage output.

It's separate from the main battle engine because bear combat uses different mechanics than standard PvP. If you're competing in bear-mode events, this is the only tool that handles those rules correctly. The scoreboard ranks formations so you can pick the best configuration before the fight.

⚡ Bear Option‑A ▼

Formation optimizer specifically for a particular bear attack strategy variant. Set target formation fractions and your available inventory, and it computes the optimal split with a ranked scoreboard.

Think of it as Bear Magic's sister tool — same goal, different tactical approach. Use it when your standard bear formation isn't getting results and you want to try an alternate deployment method.
